# Recruitment Funnel Performance & Candidate Progress Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the end-to-end recruitment process using Power BI.  
The objective is to evaluate hiring performance, identify bottlenecks in the recruitment funnel, analyze source effectiveness, and provide data-driven recommendations for process improvement.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze stage-wise candidate conversion
- Identify major drop-off points in the recruitment funnel
- Evaluate department-wise hiring efficiency
- Measure source effectiveness
- Assess average and median time-to-hire
- Provide strategic recommendations based on insights

---

## Tools & Technologies Used

- Power BI
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Data Modeling
- Data Visualization
- Microsoft Excel (Data Preparation)

---

## Key Metrics

- Total Applications
- Total Hired
- Conversion Rate (%)
- Drop-off Rate (%)
- Average Time to Hire
- Median Time to Hire
- Longest Stage Duration
- Department Efficiency (%)
- Source Effectiveness (%)

## Key Insights

- Overall conversion rate stands at 28%.
- Major drop-offs occur during the screening and offer stages.
- Company Website and Referrals are the most effective hiring sources.
- HR and Finance departments show the highest hiring efficiency.
- Hiring process duration remains relatively stable across stages.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Improve screening quality to reduce early-stage drop-offs.
- Optimize offer management and approval timelines.
- Increase investment in high-performing recruitment sources.
- Standardize hiring practices across departments.
- Continuously monitor KPIs for data-driven decision-making.

---

## Outcome

The dashboard transforms recruitment data into actionable insights, enabling strategic workforce planning and improved hiring efficiency.
